What are the benefits of being in nature?

The term 'nature' can have various meanings ranging from green spaces such as parks, woodlands, and forests, to beaches, rivers and lakes. Recent studies have proven that a simple act such as watching a nature documentary can also have positive effects on our mental health, making the benefits of nature available to all of us.
