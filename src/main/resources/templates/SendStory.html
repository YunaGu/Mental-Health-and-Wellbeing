Send us Your Story

What is a story

Sometimes the best mental health advice we get is not from professionals but from people who've been through the same thing as us. That's why we want you to share your experience in a mental health story.

How to send story

We're really glad that you've decided to send us your mental health story. At Tidy Butt we know that real people's experiences help people facing mental health problems.
