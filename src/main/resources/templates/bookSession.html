How to get Help

We have lots of information to help you understand and manage your situation when you need help.

  • If you feel like you might attempt suicide, or may have seriously harmed yourself, you should get urgent medical help.
  • If you are in crisis right now and looking for ways to help yourself stay safe.
  • If you would like to speak to one of our team directly.
  • If you need any mental health guidance.
  • If you are struggling with your mental health, psychological wellbeing hidden wounds.
